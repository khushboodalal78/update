FAQ

How much does INTERHAUL LOGISTICS charge?

We charge 8% dispatch fee

Does your fee include FACTORING?

Yes, the 8% dispatch fee that we charge includes FACTORING

Do you guys show us the RATE CONFIRMATION?

Always, as soon as we book a load, we send you the original RATE CONFIRMATION

What kind of support will you provide us?

In addition to finding the best loads for you, we help you with TRIP-PLANNING, LOGBOOK ISSUES, HOURS-OF-SERVICE RULES, SAFETY/COMPLIANCE GUIDANCE

Do I need a MC number, or can I work under INTERHAUL LOGISTICS authority?

Both. You can choose to work under your own authority (MC/DOT), or you choose to work under INTERHAUL LOGISTICS authority (MC/DOT)
